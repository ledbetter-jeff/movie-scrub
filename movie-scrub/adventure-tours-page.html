Bangtail and Grassy Mountain voted Best Rides of 2017

When you really want to push yourself and test the limits of your bike, dare to ride Bangtail. You'll need to plan for about 3-4 hours and it's advised to hit the trail no later than 9AM. Avoiding the arid summer heat when approaching the climax of this ride is essential. Keep an eye out for hikers and horse backs. As long as you come prepared, this will be one of your most epic rides ever!

Whether you're just getting started or easing back into the saddle, Grassy Mountain is a dream ride. You'll experience nearly all the same thrilling views as on Bangtail with a much less taxing ride. Watch out for truckers on the flat gravel roads. Don't forget the sun tan lotion as nearly 60% of your ride will be under the sun. From start to finish you'll need 2-3 hours.
